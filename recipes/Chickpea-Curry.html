Chickpea-Curry

Ingredients

  • 2 small cans or 1 large can of chickpeas
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 1 onion
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic
  • 120g rice or couscous
  • A splash of lime or lemon concentrate
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 2 teaspoons curry powder
  • A pinch of cinnamon
  • Sambal Oelek
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Start by cooking the rice.
  2. While the rice is cooking, chop the onion and saut√© it with the pressed garlic until translucent.
  3. Add the spices and briefly fry them with the onions.
  4. Then add the diced tomatoes and coconut milk and let everything simmer for a short time.
  5. Rinse the chickpeas and add them to the pot.
  6. Let everything simmer for about 10 minutes. Then add a splash of lime or lemon concentrate, salt, and Sambal Oelek to taste.
  7. By now, the rice should be done.

And that's it, enjoy your meal!
