Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 200g wheat flour
  • At least 10g ground flaxseeds or chia seeds
  • 250ml oat milk (I usually start with this amount and adjust the consistency as needed)
  • About 20g sugar (just enough to make it slightly sweet)

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients together.
  2. The batter should be slightly thick so that it spreads well in the pan but still holds together.
  3. Ideally, let the batter sit for a few minutes so the flax or chia seeds can expand and help bind the mixture.
  4. TI usually add a little oil to the pan after every second pancake.

And that's it, enjoy your meal!
